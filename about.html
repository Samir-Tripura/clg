About SITM

Scholar's Institute of Technology and Management is promoted by the Scholar's Academy Education Trust, founded on 8th May, 2008 under the Indian Trust Act, 1882.


Spread over a lush green expanse of more than 10 acres, set against picturesque surroundings, within a vibrant city as well as outside of it, endowed with all the modern amenities, yet in a natural amphitheatre, SITM provides an excellent academic ambience for intellectual activities.

SITM is located in the greenbelt of Guwahati, the gateway to North East India. It is only 30 minutes away from Guwahati International Airport , 5 minutes from ISBT & 40 minutes from Guwahati Railway Station.

CAMPUS MUSINGS:



Cradled alongside the blue hill and red river, the beautiful city of Guwahati (Assam) presents the perfect amphitheater to become regional light house or the educational super hub.

Spread over a lush green expanse of more than 10 acres, set against picturesque surroundings, within a vibrant city as well as outside of it, endowed with all the modern amenities, yet in a natural amphitheater, SITM provides an excellent academic ambience for intellectual pursuit. The aesthetically designed campus has a series of open spaces where the build up area and landscaped areas do not overlap with one another, rather they merge seamlessly to create an environment which enhances the development of the students personality in all respects, mental, physical and spiritual.

SITM, equipped with state-of-the-art infrastructure, contemporary academic programmes commensurate with latest developments, a team of highly qualified, motivated and competent faculty supported by most efficient, responsible and pro-active management regime, prepare its students for the next century by helping them evolve into well rounded professionals with skill and competence to spearhead transformation in the global competitive environment of 21st century. That is why students passing from SITM will be a force to reckon with in the fierce and competitive field of Science and Technology, where every day there is a new innovation and revolution changing the very essence of life. Nothing is impossible and unattainable only if you dare to nurture a dream and if you dare to do so, then, SITM is where you want to be ..... Dream big with SITM and we will be with you in your journey to success and achievement.
